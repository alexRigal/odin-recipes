Death Sandwich

Description

Deadly if not eaten properly.

You can't sue me.

Ingredients

  • White bread sub
  • Sauce
  • Ham
  • Meatballs

Steps to follow

  1. Backflip to your kitchen.
  2. Karate-chop the sub to open it.
  3. Theatrically spread some sauce, cover with slices of ham and meatballs on them.
  4. Kick the the upper half of the bread to put it in place.
  5. Wrap it in paper while breathing kung-fuesquely.
  6. Salute your sandwich.
  7. Dress up with your best cut-offs and grow a mighty mullet.
  8. Enjoy
