Ralph Wiggum's Grilled Crayon Sandwich

Description

Ralph Wiggum's legendary sandwich.

The thumbtack aftertaste is just genius.

Ingredients

  • A box of crayons
  • 2 slices of bread.
  • Thumbtacks
  • Butter

Steps to follow

  1. Drop the crayons on a slice of bread.
  2. Sprinkle some thumbtacks.
  3. Assemble.
  4. Spread some butter on the bread.
  5. Grill a couple of minutes on both sides.
  6. Make your father take a bite.
  7. Let him know about the thumbtacks later.
